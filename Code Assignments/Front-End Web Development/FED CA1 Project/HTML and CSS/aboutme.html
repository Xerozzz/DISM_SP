About Me

Hello There! I am Lee Yi Terng.
I was born in 2002 and live in Singapore! I am currently studying Diploma in Infocomm Security Management in Singapore Polytechnic. I was born in August 2002, and my horoscope is a Leo! I am a very outgoing and extroverted person who has no problems talking and meeting new people! As a result, I love socializing and making new friends!

Since I was young, I've always loved football and supported Manchester United Football Club thanks to the influence of one of my uncles. I am an avid MUFC fan and support them in their highs and lows. I am rather proud of my collection of every home jersey since 2005, and owning five Manchester United footballs as well!

I play football as a hobby too. Since young I've always waited for the recess bell to ring so that I could sprint to the field and kick around with my classmates. Nowadays, I usually play on friday nights with a few groups of adults.

Another one of my hobbies is music, both making and listening to it! I play the trumpet and perform in symphonic bands and orchestras. More on that here. I love listening to most genres of music, such as classical, pop, instrumental, vocals, rock and many more! Some of my favourite composers include Dvorak, Haydn, Mahler. I don't really have a favorite pop artist.
